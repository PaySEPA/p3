PaySwarm enables people that create digital content such as blog posts, music, film, episodic content, photos, virtual goods, and documents to distribute their creations through their website and receive payment directly from their fans and customers. It is an open, patent and royalty free, universal payment standard for the Web. If you have a passion for creating things on the Web, or would like to support people doing great things - PaySwarm is for you. If you would like to learn more about PaySwarm, the official community website is a good place to start.
